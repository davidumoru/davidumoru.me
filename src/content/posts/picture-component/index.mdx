**Source:** [Picture.astro](https://github.com/davidumoru/davidumoru.me/blob/main/src/components/Picture.astro)

## Grid

```astro
<Picture
  img={[image1, image2]}
  imgAlt={["Before", "After"]}
  variant="grid"
  columns={2}
/>
```

<Picture
  img={[exampleGrid1, exampleGrid2]}
  imgAlt={["First image in grid", "Second image in grid"]}
  variant="grid"
  columns={2}
/>

`columns={2}` and `columns={3}` supported. Collapses to one column on small screens.
